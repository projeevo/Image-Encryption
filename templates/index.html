How It Works

  1. Upload: Choose an image to upload.
  2. Select ROI: Draw a region of interest (ROI) on the image.
  3. Encrypt: Click 'Encrypt ROI' to apply chaotic encryption to the selected region.
  4. Decrypt: Use 'Decrypt ROI' to restore the original region.
  5. Metrics: View security metrics like Entropy and PSNR.
